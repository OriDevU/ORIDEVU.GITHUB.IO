To Revere an Owl



"To Revere an Owl" is an experimental novel featuring a readable owl in the shape of a book with artworks of the memories of an owl, yet the owl is unhappy about the fact they are even thought of in any manner, they want to be seen as a fierce, vicious, yet a demure and rational keeper of peace. But humans keep on covering the owl in their own imagery..
